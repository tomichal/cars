How to search for a car

  1. You must enter your postcode.
  2. Click the drop down boxes to narrow your search. You can select criteria in any order – from make and model, price, fuel, engine size, number of doors, transmission, age, mileage, trade and private adverts.
  3. Click "show results below" to display cars for sale.
  4. Compare cars by clicking "add to compare list" and "view compare list".
  5. For specific searches, type relevant words into the "keywords" box. For example, "CD player", "sport" or "leather seats".

If you're still stuck, see the Search Tip FAQs or watch our search tutorial and simply click "clear selection" to start your search again.

How do I search for a new car?

  1. Click the "NEW Cars" tab to change pages.
  2. Follow the on-screen advice.
